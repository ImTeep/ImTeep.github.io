Aerospace Engineering Masters Degree

As a part of Auburn's Space Technology & Application Research (STAR) Lab led by Dr. Masatoshi Hirabayashi, I focused on simulating impacts on Mercury (and some asteroids) to determine where escaped ejecta ends up.

Research

I am currently working with Dr. Masatoshi Hirabayashi to create an impulse-based Discrete Element Method (iDEM) simulation code to better understand how cosmic-dust will interact and be detected by BepiColombo's Mercury Dust Monitor (MDM).
